Grilled Cheese

credit: DOTDASH MEREDITH FOOD STUDIO & allrecipes.com

Description

It's Grilled Cheese!

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter one side of bread, place butter side face down in skillet and add 1 slice of cheese
  2. Repeat step one with second slice of bread
  3. Cook one side until brown, flip and cook till cheese is melted.
